1. Executive Summary

Business Overview

Gadget Fix is a locally-owned phone repair shop located at the Seattle Exchange in Southcenter, serving Tukwila, WA and the greater South Seattle metro area. We specialize in fast, affordable iPhone screen repairs with 15-30 minute turnaround times.

The Trade-In Arbitrage Opportunity

We have identified a significant market opportunity: customers with damaged phones lose hundreds of dollars in trade-in value. By repairing screens before trade-in, customers can profit from the value difference.

Carrier Partnership Strategy

We target carrier store employees with a "Save-the-Sale" value proposition:

The Partner Pitch

When a customer is denied a trade-in due to screen damage, the carrier rep loses their commission. Gadget Fix repairs the phone in 30 minutes, allowing the customer to return same-day and complete the upgrade. The rep saves their sale, the customer profits, and we earn the repair.

9. Risk Assessment

Risk Matrix

Risk Probability Impact Mitigation
Trade-in programs eliminated Low High Diversify messaging beyond trade-in value
New competitor enters market Medium Medium Lock in partnerships, build brand loyalty
Supply chain disruption Medium High Maintain 2-3 supplier relationships
Owner unable to work Low Critical Business continuity plan (see below)
Marketplace rent increase Medium Medium Maintain good vendor relationship, explore alternatives

Business Continuity Plan

If Owner is Temporarily Unable to Work
  • Backup Technician: On-call agreement with Isaac (Gadget Rehab, Seattle) for emergency coverage
  • Process Documentation: All repair SOPs documented for temporary staff
  • Customer Communication: Voicemail/text auto-reply with expected return date
  • Partnership Continuity: Carrier partners notified with timeline

Risk Mitigation Priorities

Immediate Actions
  • Maintain 2-3 supplier relationships
  • Build 3-month expense reserve
  • Document all repair processes
Ongoing Actions
  • Track marketing ROI monthly
  • Diversify marketing channels
  • Maintain marketplace relationship
