Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a screen repair take?
Most iPhone screen repairs are completed in just 15-30 minutes while you wait. For more complex repairs or older models, it may take up to an hour. We'll give you an accurate time estimate when you bring in your device.
Do you use original Apple parts?
We use high-quality aftermarket parts that match OEM specifications. These parts look, feel, and perform like original Apple components but at a much lower cost. All parts come with our 90-day warranty.
Will fixing my screen really increase trade-in value?
Yes! We've researched every major carrier and Apple's trade-in program. A cracked screen can reduce your trade-in value by $300-$500. A $99-$179 repair can literally put hundreds of dollars back in your pocket. We're happy to show you the exact numbers for your specific phone model.
What's covered under your warranty?
Our 90-day warranty covers any defects in the replacement parts or workmanship. If your new screen develops issues like dead pixels, touch problems, or display glitches that aren't caused by new damage, we'll fix it for free.
Do I need an appointment?
No appointment needed! Walk-ins are always welcome. We're open 7 days a week from 11am to 7pm. For the fastest service, feel free to call ahead at (425) 666-9090 and we can have your parts ready when you arrive.
Will I lose my data during the repair?
No! Screen and battery replacements do not affect your data at all. Your photos, messages, apps, and settings will remain exactly as they were. We never need your passcode for these repairs.
